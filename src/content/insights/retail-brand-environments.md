Retail and brand environments fail when display drama comes at the cost of operations. Service routes, storage, and MEP maintenance access must be resolved in the first planning week — not after ceiling grids are locked.

## Display lighting as structure

We coordinate lighting positions with ceiling services before grid submission. Product focus points drive circuiting — not the other way around.

## Customer journey vs. staff flow

The sales path should feel intuitive; staff and stock movement should never cross it visibly. We separate back-of-house circulation early in layout studies.

## Launch-critical schedules

Brand openings run on immovable dates. Parallel procurement lanes and pre-fabricated joinery compress programmes without sacrificing finish quality.
